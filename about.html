Attendance Reports

Download attendance report with student phone status and absence reason.

About This System

AI Powered Attendance Monitoring System is developed to automatically contact parents when a student is absent. The system calls parents and records the reason for absence using speech-to-text technology.

Why This System Was Developed

Schools often face difficulty in collecting absence reasons from parents. This system automates the process using AI voice calls and converts the parent's speech into text which is displayed on the dashboard.
